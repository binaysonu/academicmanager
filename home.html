About Our College

Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi

East West College of Engineering established in 2015 with the prime motto to serve the best quality-based technical education. The campus is advantageously located in the well-known green city of Bengaluru and has an extensive vicinity of 5 acres of land surrounded by a serene and pleasant learning atmosphere.

EWCE has always stood for discipline and the molding of character of its students. Our college prides itself on imparting quality education by providing a strong foundation for holistic learning. East West College of Engineering stands testimony to the high professional standards aimed for and achieved by the EWGI.

East West College of Engineering - A proud limb of East West Group of Institutions, Bengaluru.
