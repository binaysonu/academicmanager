About Us

East West Group of Institutions

EAST WEST GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS, a well-known and prominent educational institution, holds a distinguished place among the top colleges in Bengaluru. Founded by philanthropist and social worker Shri. C M Nagaraj in 1968, it has earned a reputation for excellence in technological education, attracting students from around the globe.

Celebrating 52 years of academic excellence, EWGI fosters holistic development by offering diverse programs such as Engineering, Architecture, Science & Commerce, Management, Pharmacy, Polytechnic, Nursing, and more.

East West College of Engineering

Located in Bengaluru's serene environment, EWCE covers five acres of lush greenery. It offers branches including Aeronautical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Agricultural Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering, each with a 60-student intake.

The institution nurtures professional and personal skills while fostering civic responsibility through industry-academic collaborations and continuous career development programs.
